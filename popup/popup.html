About OIS

It annoyed me that the Steam website requires you to log in nearly daily (it seems).

There's also no preset buttons to open pages in the Steam client would make life easier for a few of us, so that's where OIS comes into play.

Unfortunately, I cannot get everything to open in the Steam client, for example, Steam forums or app discussion pages (at least I haven't found a way, but if you do know a method, please let me know!).

If you find any bugs, please report them to me with details of it and the page you were on at the time if relevant.
